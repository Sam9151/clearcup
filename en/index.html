Frequently asked questions

What is drink spiking?

Drink spiking involves adding a psychoactive substance to someone's drink without their knowledge in order to impair their consciousness. GHB is the most commonly used molecule.

How does the ClearCup sticker work?

The ClearCup sticker is a food-grade vinyl sticker that adheres over the top of a glass. It is pre-perforated to allow a straw through, preventing any substance from being slipped into the drink without the guest's knowledge.

Is ClearCup suitable for bars and clubs?

Yes. ClearCup is a B2B solution designed for nightlife venues, bars, clubs, restaurants and festival organizers. We offer volume pricing and dedicated sales support.

Is the sticker suitable for festivals?

Absolutely. The ClearCup sticker is lightweight, compact and easy to distribute at scale, making it an ideal solution for festivals and large events such as the Street Parade Zurich (800,000 attendees), Paléo Festival Nyon (200,000 attendees) or Openair St. Gallen (110,000 attendees).
