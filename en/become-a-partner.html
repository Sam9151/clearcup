Become a ClearCup Partner
in Switzerland

ClearCup is the anti-drink spiking glass protection solution built for nightlife professionals and event organizers across Switzerland. We work directly with bars, clubs and festival organizers — in both French-speaking and German-speaking Switzerland — who want to integrate a concrete prevention approach into their operations.

Why partner with ClearCup?

A visible commitment

Every ClearCup sticker you distribute is a strong signal: your venue takes safety seriously. In an industry where reputation increasingly rests on these commitments, it's a genuine differentiator.

Zero operational friction

No items to collect, no washing, no complex inventory to manage. The sticker integrates naturally into your service workflow. Your team picks it up in under an hour.

A local Swiss supplier

ClearCup is based in Fribourg. Orders managed in Switzerland, fast delivery across the country, with a dedicated contact available in French, German and English. No import delays from France or abroad.

Adapted B2B pricing

Volume-based pricing schedule, adapted to venues of all sizes. An 80-seat bar orders differently from a festival with 50,000 attendees — we adapt the offer to both.

Who we work with

Bars and cafes

You serve drinks every night. Integrating ClearCup into your service protects your guests on every drink served, without adding weight to your bartenders' workflow. Ideal for bars in Zurich, Geneva, Lausanne, Bern, Basel, Fribourg and beyond.

Clubs and nightlife venues

The late-night environment concentrates the risks: low lighting, large crowds, loud music. Drink protection in clubs is one of the most effective measures to reduce drink spiking opportunities. ClearCup integrates at the main bar and secondary bars alike.

Festivals and events

Paléo Festival Nyon (200,000 attendees), Street Parade Zurich (800,000 attendees, 2025), Gurtenfestival Bern (98,000 attendees, 2025), Openair Frauenfeld (87,000 attendees, 2025), Montreux Jazz Festival, Openair St. Gallen (110,000 attendees) — and any Swiss event looking to deploy glass protection at scale. We handle volume orders with advance delivery before your event.

Student associations and parties

Student events are an identified risk environment. Associations from UNIL, EPFL, University of Fribourg, HES-SO — ClearCup offers pricing adapted to association budgets.

How to get started

  1. 01

    Get in touch

    Send an email to info@clearcup.ch specifying the type of venue, your location in Switzerland, and an estimated volume per week or per event.

  2. 02

    Receive your quote

    We'll send you a B2B pricing proposal within 48 business hours.

  3. 03

    Place your first order

    Minimum order adapted to your size. Delivery across Switzerland within 5 to 7 business days.

  4. 04

    Integrate ClearCup into your service

    Staff briefing included: we provide an onboarding sheet explaining the service gesture and the message to communicate to your guests.
